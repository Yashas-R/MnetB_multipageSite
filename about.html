Our Journey

2019

Bengaluru, India

The Garage Begins

Three engineers, one frustrating commute, and a borrowed workshop. VoltRide's first prototype is assembled in a Koramangala garage.

2020

First 100 Riders

We launch the VOLT URBAN X1 beta. 100 riders sign up in 48 hours. Their feedback reshapes every component we thought was "finished."

2022

Series A & Scale

₹42 crore raised. New manufacturing facility in Pune. Team grows from 8 to 85. The APEX TRAIL PRO launches to instant acclaim.

2024

10,000 Bikes on Road

VoltRide crosses 10,000 bikes sold across 28 Indian cities. The PHANTOM GT-R becomes our halo product. Service centres open in 12 cities.

2025

The Future

Solid-state battery prototypes. Solar charging integration. VoltRide Community App. The ride has only just begun.
